Reductionism is the view that complex systems can be understood by breaking them down into their constituent parts.

A reductionist approach emphasizes:
- Analysis and decomposition
- Understanding mechanisms at lower levels
- Causal relationships between components

Related: [Systemism](/notes/systemism), [Holism](/notes/holism)
